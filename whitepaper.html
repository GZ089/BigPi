Introduction

BigPi is a revolutionary blockchain-based ecosystem designed to create a decentralized economy where users can participate in mining, complete tasks, and contribute to network growth while being rewarded fairly.

This document outlines the tokenomics behind the BigPi ecosystem, explaining the distribution, utility, and economic principles that guide the project's sustainable growth.
